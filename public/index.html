    How It Works

    Connect your X4 via USB, click the flash button, and select your device. The firmware is written to the backup OTA partition, then the device reboots into the new firmware. Your settings and books are preserved.

    Make sure your X4 is powered on and connected via USB before flashing. Only Chrome and Edge support WebSerial.
